## 💡 LIGHTING SYSTEM

### Clean, Professional Lighting

**Setup:**
1. **Ambient**: 0.6 intensity (soft base)
2. **Key Light**: [5, 8, 5], intensity 1.5 (main + shadows)
3. **Fill Light**: [-5, 5, -5], intensity 0.8 (fill shadows)
4. **Top Light**: [0, 5, 0], intensity 1.0, blue tint

**Shadow Quality:**
- 2048x2048 shadow maps
- Soft contact shadows
- Professional appearance

**Environment:**
- City preset (clean reflections)
- Subtle environment mapping
- Professional look
